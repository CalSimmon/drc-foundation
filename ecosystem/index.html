Governance and Proposals

The DRC Foundation was created to establish a community-driven governance of the DRC ecosystem. We believe that the most important decisions regarding the development of the DRC ecosystem should be made exclusively by DRC token holders. Holders of DRC can create new proposals and vote on any changes to DRC and its ecosystem at DRC Foundation Snapshot

The governance system of DRC is truly unique because we do not have managers and even core contributors. No one has managerial control over DRC network. Our goal is to create a decentralized ecosystem, to the extent possible. We believe this will make DRC a compliant and at the same time, an autonomous and censorship-resistant digital store of value.
